About

The Department of Physical Education was formed in the year 2003 with one Physical Education Instructor. In August 2007, Mr. Krishnakumar S., the current Head of the Department (HoD) took charge of the department. The department coordinates the sports activities of all the schools at Amritapuri Campus i.e., School of Arts & Science, School of Ayurveda, School of Biotechnology and School of Engineering. The Department regularly organizes various intramural competitions and annual athletic meets. Various Inter Campus Tournaments including Table Tennis, Badminton (Shuttle), Volleyball, Football, etc. are also organized year-round.

BASKET BALL

The resounding success of the Amritapuri Campus Men's Basketball Team in securing the championship title at the 5th Phase of the Inter-Campus Basketball competition, hosted at AIMS (Amrita Institute of Medical Science) Kochi Campus from December 8th to 11th, 2022, stands as a testament to their exceptional skill, teamwork, and unwavering determination. This achievement not only highlights the prowess of the Men's Basketball Team but also underscores the supportive and encouraging sports culture fostered at Amritapuri Campus. Congratulations to the team for their outstanding performance and for bringing honor and pride to the campus in the Inter-Campus Basketball competition. The victory stands as a symbol of the vibrant sports spirit and competitive edge nurtured within the Amrita family.

CHESS

The exceptional performance of both the Men and Women teams from Amritapuri Campus during the 3rd Phase of the Inter-Campus Chess Tournament at Amritapuri Campus on November 26th and 27th, 2022, culminated in a well-deserved 2nd position. Their strategic prowess and dedication showcased not only individual skill but also a remarkable team spirit that contributed to this impressive achievement. The chess enthusiasts from Amritapuri Campus displayed sportsmanship, perseverance, and strategic brilliance throughout the competition, earning them recognition and applause. This achievement is a testament to the commitment and talent of the chess teams, reflecting positively on the sports culture and competitive spirit at Amritapuri Campus. Congratulations to the Men and Women teams for their outstanding performance in the tournament.
